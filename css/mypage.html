My Nauvoo Performing Mission in 2021



What missionary work did I do in Nauvoo?

Singing, dancing, and acting was our mission language! I was grouped with a few dosen of the most talented, bright, and grounded young people in the Church. We perfromed all kinds of shows like vignettes, dance numbers, musicals, concerts, and set pieces. Performing Missionaries were called to be stage, band, or tech missionaries (depending on the applicaion). The duration of my performing mission in Historic Nauvoo was from May to October 2021.



Mapping my Daily Performances

      Performances are listed from the beginning to the end of each day. Groups of missionaries would rotate performances throughout the week.

    • "Just Plain Anna Amanda" - Cultural Hall (SW corner of Main and White St.)
    • "Hymns of Zion" - Cultural Hall (SW corner of Main and White St.)
    • "Outdoor Set" - Singing Tree (South of Browning Home on Main St.)
    • "Go Ye Into All the World" - Seventies Hall (Parley St. East of Trail of Hope)
    • "The Promise" - Historic Nauvoo Visitors' Center (Main St. farther North)
    • "Sunset By the Mississippi" - Outdoor Stage (East of Historic Nauvoo Visitors' Center)
    • "Trial of Hope" - Trail of Hope (Parley St. West of Seventies Hall)

      • Occasionally, a singing tree group would drive to Carthage to perform a set of Hymns there!
